Others build an AI that talks about your money.
Sentinel builds an AI team that watches, predicts, plans, proves it's compliant, explains itself, and waits for your yes.

SBI Sentinel03 · The reframe
The solution

SBI Sentinel, an agentic wellbeing layer inside YONO

Eight specialist AI agents, orchestrated by a supervisor, run a continuous closed loop on each customer, grounded in SBI product knowledge and RBI/DPDP rules.

👁️

Detect & Predict

Continuously scores wellbeing and forecasts risk, an EMI bounce, a liquidity crunch, a fraud pattern, days ahead.

🧭

Plan & Simulate

Generates 2-3 personalised options and simulates each plan's future impact on the customer's score and cashflow.

⚖️

Prove & Explain

A deterministic engine certifies every plan against RBI/DPDP; an explainability ledger shows the evidence.

✔ Non-negotiable

The AI never moves money on its own. Every action is consent-gated, reversible, executed on existing SBI rails, and written to an immutable audit ledger. Agent proposes, customer approves, bank executes.

SBI Sentinel07 · Financial Wellbeing Score
The moat

Any LLM can suggest. Sentinel proves it's safe and shows its work.

① Compliance-Validated Plan

A deterministic rule engine (not the LLM) checks every plan against RBI conduct rules, DPDP consent, suitability & mis-selling. It issues a Compliance Certificate, or a VETO. Unsafe advice never reaches the customer.

✔ Suitability OK✔ No mis-sell✔ Consent valid

② Explainability Ledger

Every recommendation carries its confidence, the exact evidence transactions, the reasoning trail, and the certificate, a plain-language story for the customer and a tamper-evident record for the auditor.

Why it's defensible: replicating this needs banking-grade regulatory grounding + a governed tool layer + an audit spine, not just a clever prompt. That's a moat a weekend fintech can't cross.

Roadmap

From prototype to 50 crore customers

Now · 0-3 months

Prototype the golden-path loop: FWS engine, Risk + Planner + Compliance + Explainability agents, HITL approve/execute, one YONO-embedded journey. Pilot on a synthetic cohort.

Next · 3-12 months

All 8 agents, fraud + insurance/investment gaps, Neo4j customer-360, Langfuse eval harness, RBI-sandbox pilot with a real branch cohort, DPDP consent ledger live.

Later · 12-24 months

Full YONO rollout, multilingual voice (Bhashini), household-level planning, tax optimization, and B2B2C licensing to cooperative banks & NBFCs.
